🔒 Privacy Policy

Papir Card System

Last Updated: March 2025
Transparency Promise: We collect minimal data necessary to provide our Service. We never sell your personal information. Our data retention practices follow a tiered approach designed to balance functionality, privacy, and legal compliance.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Data Type Purpose Required
Email Address Account creation, notifications For registered users
Uploaded Media To display on your AR cards Yes, for card creation
Card Metadata File names, sizes, types Automatic with uploads
Payment Information Processing subscriptions For paid plans only
Terms Acceptance Legal record of consent For card activation

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • IP Address: For security, analytics, and legal records
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Card scans, feature usage, error logs
  • Location Data: Approximate location from IP (country/city level)

1.3 QR Code Scan Data

When someone scans your QR code, we collect:

  • Timestamp of scan
  • Scanner's IP address (for analytics and fraud prevention)
  • Browser/device type
  • No personal identification of scanner

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Access & Correction

You can access and update your account information through your profile settings.

6.2 Data Export

Request an export of your data by contacting privacy@papir.ca. We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

6.3 Data Deletion

Delete your account anytime through settings or by email request. Note:

  • Card content will be deleted immediately
  • Scan logs will be anonymized (cannot be linked back to you)
  • Activation IP records are permanently retained for legal compliance (proof of terms acceptance)
  • Public QR codes may remain accessible until cache expires

6.4 Opt-Out Options

  • Marketing emails: Unsubscribe link in all emails
  • Analytics: Use browser "Do Not Track" settings
  • Cookies: Manage through browser settings

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies:

  • Session Cookies: For login functionality (expire when browser closes)
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous usage statistics (30-day retention)
  • No advertising or tracking cookies

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.
